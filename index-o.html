bio

Who is Luis?

I am a Berlin-based front-end developer and designer. After 7 years working as a graphic designer I started learning how to code and changed my career path. Now I am focused on front-end development in JavaScript and ReactJS.

I have a simple and bold approach to coding and design, always looking for the simplest solution to a problem.

Aside from my new love for coding, my passions are typography, illustration, neatly organized layouts, practical philosophy, history, tropical music, and home-made food.

When I am not coding or learning something new, I like doing illustration projects and spending time with family and friends.

If you are looking for a nice front-end developer, team-player and eager to learn, get in touch!

Three reasons to hire me

Problem Solver

The main goal of a graphic designer is to solve visual problems through form, type, color and space. I use the same approach into web development: I try to find creative solutions in the simplest possible way.

Rapid Learner

I am a very curious person and an avid learner. As trends and technology continuously change, it is very important to keep learning constantly. My willingness to learn helps me being a better developer each day.

Communication Skills

As a fellow graphic designer myself, I speak the language of designers and project managers. Due to experience I already hold the skill of effective communication and can apply it into future projects.

These are my values

01/ I like to follow Arthur Ashe’s advice: “Start where you are, use what you have, do what you can”.

02/ Think first, make a plan and act accordingly. If the plan is not working, plan again.

03/ I am a minimalist at heart. I seek for ways to do more with less.
